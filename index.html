Gangstas to Growers (G2G) is a social enterprise focused on building worker-owned cooperatives that provide opportunities for employment, empowerment, and entrepreneurship in agriculture for at-risk youth and formerly incarcerated individuals.

Gangstas to Growers: Non-Profit Web & Mobile Responsive Design Challenge

Roles: User Researcher, Information Architect, UX & UI Designer Timeline: 3 Week Sprint - March 23-April 10, 2021 Collaborators: Nick Duncan, Abby Nelson, John Hand, Sylvia McCoy Tools: Miro, Figma, InVision

THE PROBLEM: The Gangstas to Growers current website was intended to be a portal allowing community-invested donors to feel connected to the local cause & make contributions.

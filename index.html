Gangstas to Growers (G2G) is a social enterprise focused on building worker-owned cooperatives that provide opportunities for employment, empowerment, and entrepreneurship in agriculture for at-risk youth and formerly incarcerated individuals.

Gangstas to Growers: Non-Profit Web & Mobile Responsive Design Challenge

Roles: User Researcher, Information Architect, UX & UI Designer Timeline: 3 Week Sprint - March 23-April 10, 2021 Collaborators: Nick Duncan, Abby Nelson, John Hand, Sylvia McCoy Tools: Miro, Figma, InVision

THE PROBLEM: The Gangstas to Growers current website was intended to be a portal allowing community-invested donors to feel connected to the local cause & make contributions. Key User Insights

The first key insight we uncovered was that users notice a key lack of transpareny on the G2G website. They were looking to read more about thier story, how they got started. They wanted to know more about the program what projects they are currently focused on.

Secondly, users not only wanted to know more about the story of Gangters to Growers, but they wanted to hear the stories of students who have been through the program. And to add, they wanted to know the story of the Sweet Sol Hot Sauce that G2G sells. None of this information was front and cetner on the site.

Lastly, a major issue was that there was no way for donors to make a gift on the homepage. The was no information about how to get involved and help the cause, and no information on how to enroll participants. This is a key factor in promoting the future of G2G.

Final Thoughts

This project was challenging in the aspect that the stakeholder required us to maintain the current asthetics of their site, while meeting the needs of users. It was important however that we made contact with G2G and had them bought in from an early point. In the future we wouldl love to test some of our new features and see if they make a difference in donations and creating awareness for the brand. We would love to incorporate social feeds and a full web store in the future.
